Weekly and monthly subscription plans

What is monthly/weekly subscription?

Weekly subscription |

⁃ 7% discount on total price.
⁃ Minimal of 7 meals plan (more is ok)
⁃ Subscription finishes in 7 days.
⁃ Delivery 1,000 ID per order only

Monthly subscription |

⁃ 12% discount on total price.
⁃ Minimal of 30 meals plan (more is ok)
⁃ Subscription finishes in 30 days.
⁃ Delivery 1,000 ID per order only

How to have weekly/monthly subscription?

There are two way:
1. Setting weekly/monthly plan: this can be beneficial if you can make a schedule of the meals that you wanna eat through out the period of subscription.
2. Credits: this would be beneficial if you want to subscribe but cant decide what you wanna eat in advance in the period of your subscription.
